What is our Purpose?

Getting good quality high definition audio is key to some peoples lives and we are here to remedy the issue of terrible microphones.

we get microphones directly from MAONO, a very high quality microphone manufacturer. We pick up some of their normal units and distribute them to the local area at a lower price.

We work closely to MAONO and hope to improve the lives of some of our users through our audio quality and mic durability.
